About Me

I am Saleh Hamed Alrashidi, a passionate and dedicated software engineer with a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from the University of Hail. My journey in the field has been enriched by hands-on experiences and diverse projects, allowing me to become proficient in various technologies such as Python, Django, React, SQL Server, and Firebase. I am currently a Python Web Developer trainee at Tuwaiq Academy in Riyadh, where I am further honing my skills through an intensive bootcamp that focuses on building real-world web applications, mastering version control, and deployment strategies. Prior to this, I completed an internship at King Fahad Medical City, where I deepened my knowledge in C# and data analysis tools like Power BI. Additionally, my time as a programming member at PGC involved leading projects, conducting workshops, and fostering a collaborative learning environment. Adaptability, problem-solving, teamwork, and a commitment to continuous learning are at the heart of my professional ethos. With fluency in both Arabic and English, I aim to contribute meaningfully to the tech landscape, creating impactful and scalable solutions.
